About

We open access to hospitality that has traditionally been out of reach - allowing people to own a piece of exceptional hotels and resorts.

ventoré creates design-led hotels and resorts in places that already carry weight. From €40,000, you step into fractional ownership from the earliest stage of a project. Every project is built as a complete system - where design, positioning, and demand are considered from the start. We don't separate vision from execution, or product from marketing. All ventoré projects are designed for a modern audience: visually distinctive, digitally native, and inherently shareable.

This leads to:

  • Higher volumes of direct bookings
  • Greater control over pricing and demand
  • Stronger, more stable performance over time

The Process
+

[1] Submit Your Details and Schedule a 30 Minute Call with Our Team

We'll understand your goals, walk you through the model, and explore which projects may be the right fit.

[2] Explore the Opportunity

If there's a strong match, you'll receive access to the project data room. Here, you can review everything in detail - including the concept, design, location thesis, and financial model.

[3] Invest with Clarity

Once you're ready, we'll guide you through the final investment call - answering any questions and supporting you through the full process, from review to onboarding.

[4] We Build and Operate

ventoré designs, develops, and manages each project end-to-end. You're connected from the earliest stage through to opening and beyond.

[5] Returns and Ongoing Access

We'll keep you updated about your project throughout. When the hotel opens, it begins generating revenue and you'll receive quarterly dividends.
