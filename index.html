About

We open access to hospitality ownerships that has traditionally been out of reach — allowing people to own a piece of exceptional hotels and resorts.

ventoré creates design-led hotels and resorts in places that already carry weight. From €40,000, you step into fractional ownership from the earliest stage of a project. Every project is built as a complete system — where design, positioning, and demand are considered from the start. We don't separate vision from execution, or product from marketing. All ventoré projects are designed for a modern audience: visually distinctive, digitally native, and inherently shareable.

Visibility is designed into every project. Each property is shaped not only for how it operates, but for how it's seen, shared, and desired. Social media plays a defining role in how hotels perform — attracting the right guests, increasing direct bookings, and reducing reliance on third-party platforms.

This leads to:

  • Higher volumes of direct bookings
  • Greater control over pricing and demand
  • Stronger, more stable performance over time

As visibility grows, desirability increases.

As demand increases, occupancy strengthens.

As more guests arrive, more content is created.

The cycle compounds, turning attention into revenue.

This allows each project to build attention early, accelerate bookings, and operate at a consistently high level from launch — turning design into distribution, and attention into revenue.

Our Co-Founders are Nils Ehrenfried and Jake Snow. Nils leads as CEO, overseeing global growth and operations, while Jake shapes the creative direction of each project. Together, they are building a new model of hospitality ownership, where product, brand, and demand are designed as one.

What defines ventoré is simple: we create places worth returning to — and a way of being part of them from the beginning.

FAQ

  • Can I sell my share?

    Yes — after a minimum holding period, shares can be transferred through ventoré or sold to approved buyers. Each project has its own terms, which we walk you through during the investment call.

  • Have you done projects before?

    Our team has designed, developed, and operated hospitality projects across multiple regions. Each ventoré project builds on this experience — bringing together design, development, and long-term operation under one model.

  • What currency?

    Investments are denominated in EUR, with USD options available for certain projects. Returns are distributed in the same currency you invest in.

  • Legal structure?

    Each project is held through a dedicated legal entity in a stable, investor-friendly jurisdiction. Ownership is fractionalised through clear, audited documentation — and reviewed by independent legal counsel.

  • Questions on risk and transparency?

    Every project comes with a full data room — including the concept, design, location thesis, financial model, and risk disclosures. We answer every question directly, before and after investment. Transparency is at the centre of how we operate.
